About Intrepid Travel

Small group adventures that change how you see the world

Our Story

Intrepid Travel began in 1989 with a simple idea: to show travelers the real world through small group adventures led by local leaders.

What started with two founders and a handful of travelers has grown into one of the world's leading adventure travel companies, taking over a million travelers to destinations across every continent.

We believe that travel is more than just visiting new places—it's about connecting with local communities, experiencing different cultures, and gaining a deeper understanding of our world.

Sustainability

We're committed to becoming a carbon neutral company by 2025 and ensuring our trips benefit local communities.

Our approach includes:

  • Carbon offsetting all our trips
  • Working with local suppliers and operators
  • Small group sizes to minimize environmental impact
  • Community projects in destinations we visit
  • Removing single-use plastics from our trips
